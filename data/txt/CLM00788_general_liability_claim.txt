Description of Incident (from claimant attorney's letter):
"Our client, Linda M. Watkins, a tenant residing at the above-referenced property,
suffered serious personal injuries on April 28, 2023, when she fell on a broken
concrete step at the main entrance stairwell of the property. Ms. Watkins alleges

repairs were made prior to the incident. As a proximate result of the fall, our
client sustained: (1) displaced fracture of the right distal radius; (2) contusion
and lacerations to right knee; (3) mild concussion. She underwent surgical fixation
of the right wrist on April 30, 2023 at Denver Health Medical Center."
